Open Positions

Postdoctoral Researchers

Join the Shin Research Group in Fall 2024.

  • Ideal candidates should have experience in one or more of the following areas: numerical optimization, numerical linear algebra, control theory, or energy system modeling and analysis. Experience in Julia Language, battery system modeling, or rigorous mathematical analysis is a plus.
  • Interested candidates should email Sungho along with their CV and a brief description of their research interests and career goals.

Graduate Students

Join the Shin Research Group in Fall 2023.

  • Prospective applicants are advised to initiate their application process with the MIT Department of Chemical Engineering. Fee waivers can be obtained through the Dean of Engineering. Additionally, we encourage first-year students in the chemical engineering program to participate in the 10.990 seminar series, which provides the opportunity to learn about different research groups at the MIT Chemical Engineering Department.
  • Ideal candidates should have the intention to tackle challenging problems with Sungho.
  • Interested students (already admitted to MIT) should email Sungho and schedule individual meetings prior to advisor selection.
